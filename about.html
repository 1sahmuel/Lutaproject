About Us

HOME / ABOUT

AT RIGHT CHOICE BARBING SALON, WE BELIEVE THAT, AT ITS BEST, A BARBER SHOP IS A DEPENDABLE, TRUSTED NEIGHBORHOOD INSTITUTION.


OUR PRIMARY GOAL IS TO BUILD STRONG, LONG-TERM RELATIONSHIPS WITH EACH OF OUR CLIENTS.

Right Choice Barbing Salon is proud to be an independent and operated store focused solely on serving its customers. Our unparalleled team of expert barbers has personal pride riding on each haircut and hot lather shave. No one on Right Choice Barbers' staff has been cutting hair fewer than 10 years. We've seen it all and can do it all. We are about substance and skill, not gimmicks. We like to have fun, but we take our work seriously.
